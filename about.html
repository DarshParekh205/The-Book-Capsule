About The Book Capsule

The Book Capsule is your cozy corner on the internet for discovering bite-sized book wisdom. Whether you're a curious learner, a busy student, or an aspiring reader, we’ve made it easier for you to explore the world of books—one page, one idea, and one summary at a time.

Our platform features thoughtfully curated summaries from a wide range of genres—fiction, non-fiction, psychology, self-help, and more. Each book capsule includes key takeaways, video summaries, and a brief look at why it’s worth your time.

This project is built with using HTML, and CSS. It’s not just a website—it’s a growing library of condensed knowledge that fits in your pocket.

Happy Reading!
