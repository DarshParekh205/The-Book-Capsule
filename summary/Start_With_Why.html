Start With Why by Simon Sinek

Start With Why explains how great leaders and organizations inspire action by starting with a clear purpose—“why” they do what they do.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. The Golden Circle: Why → How → What. Begin with why to inspire.
  • 2. Leadership is Influence: True leaders communicate their beliefs clearly.
  • 3. Trust and Loyalty: People follow leaders who share their values and vision.

This book is a guide for entrepreneurs, leaders, and anyone who wants to spark meaningful change and impact.
