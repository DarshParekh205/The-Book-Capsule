Elon Musk by Ashlee Vance

This biography showcases Elon Musk’s journey from South Africa to founding PayPal, Tesla, SpaceX, and more. It reveals his relentless work ethic, vision, and unorthodox style.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Vision is Power: Big dreams lead to world-changing innovation.
  • 2. Embrace Risk: Don’t fear failure—learn and adapt quickly.
  • 3. Work Ethic: Musk’s insane drive sets him apart.
