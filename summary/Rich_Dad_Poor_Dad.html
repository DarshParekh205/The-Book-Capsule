Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert T. Kiyosaki

Rich Dad Poor Dad contrasts the financial philosophies of two fathersâ€”one rich and one poor. Through personal stories, Robert Kiyosaki shares life-changing lessons about money, investing, and the mindset needed for financial independence.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Assets vs. Liabilities: Focus on acquiring assets that generate income rather than liabilities that take money out of your pocket.
  • 2. Financial Education: Schools teach academics, not how to manage money. Financial literacy is key to wealth.
  • 3. Work to Learn, Not to Earn: Invest time in learning skills that help you grow financially rather than just earning a paycheck.

This book is a foundational read for anyone who wants to escape the rat race, build passive income, and rethink their financial future.
