Your Money or Your Life by Vicki Robin & Joe Dominguez

Your Money or Your Life is a transformative personal finance book that helps readers rethink their relationship with money. It challenges the traditional 9-to-5 mindset and teaches how to align spending with life purpose to achieve financial independence and fulfillment.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Money = Life Energy: Every dollar you spend represents time and energy you’ve exchanged for it. Spend mindfully.
  • 2. Track Every Expense: Know where your money goes, so you can make conscious choices aligned with your values.
  • 3. Enough is Enough: The goal isn't to be rich, but to have enough—and reclaim your time and freedom.

This book isn’t just about budgeting—it’s a mindset shift. It's perfect for those who want to escape consumerism and live a more intentional, purpose-driven life.
