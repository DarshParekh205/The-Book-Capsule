Long Walk to Freedom by Nelson Mandela

This is the powerful autobiography of Nelson Mandela, chronicling his early life, education, 27 years in prison, and his role in the fight against apartheid in South Africa.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. The Power of Forgiveness: True leadership requires letting go of bitterness.
  • 2. Perseverance: Change comes through sacrifice and time.
  • 3. Equality: Every human deserves dignity and freedom.
