Wings of Fire by A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

This autobiography tells the story of Dr. Kalam’s rise from humble beginnings to becoming India’s Missile Man and President. It's a powerful message of dreaming big and staying humble.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Dream Big: Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts into action.
  • 2. Hard Work: Success is built through dedication and perseverance.
  • 3. Humility: Stay grounded regardless of your achievements.
