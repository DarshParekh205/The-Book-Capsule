Meditations by Marcus Aurelius

Meditations is a series of personal writings by Roman Emperor Marcus Aurelius, offering timeless wisdom on self-discipline, resilience, humility, and living with purpose through Stoic philosophy.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Control What You Can: Focus on your thoughts and actions, not external events.
  • 2. Live According to Nature: Accept your role in the world with virtue and reason.
  • 3. Mind Over Emotion: Stoicism teaches mastering your emotions to achieve peace.

This book is a guide to living a life of purpose and integrity regardless of circumstances.
