The 5 AM Club by Robin Sharma

The 5 AM Club by Robin Sharma is a self-help book that emphasizes the importance of rising early to achieve success, improve health, and lead a more fulfilled life. Using a storytelling format, Sharma presents a powerful routine called the 20/20/20 formula to kickstart your day with purpose and clarity.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Own Your Morning: The first hour of the day (5:00 AM to 6:00 AM) is your time to set the tone for peak performance.
  • 2. The 20/20/20 Formula: Divide the first hour into three 20-minute blocks — Move (exercise), Reflect (meditate/journal), and Grow (learn).
  • 3. The Victory Hour: This golden hour boosts productivity, creativity, and personal mastery throughout the day.

The book stresses how early rising allows you to escape distraction, build willpower, and pursue self-mastery. It’s a compelling read for anyone looking to transform their life through discipline and intentional routines.
