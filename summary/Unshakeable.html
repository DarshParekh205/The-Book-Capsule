Unshakeable by Tony Robbins

Unshakeable is Tony Robbins’ guide to mastering the financial markets with confidence and clarity. It focuses on building a long-term mindset and resisting emotional reactions to market fluctuations.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Don’t Try to Time the Market: Stay invested long-term and avoid panic selling.
  • 2. Diversify: Use low-cost index funds and diversify across asset classes.
  • 3. Control Emotions: Emotional decisions are often the most expensive ones in investing.

This book is a great starting point for building a solid mindset toward investing and becoming financially resilient.
