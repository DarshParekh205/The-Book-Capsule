Man's Search for Meaning by Viktor Frankl

Man’s Search for Meaning is a profound memoir by psychiatrist and Holocaust survivor Viktor Frankl, exploring how finding meaning gives us strength to endure life's hardships.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Meaning in Suffering: When we find meaning in our pain, we gain power to transcend it.
  • 2. Freedom of Choice: Even in extreme conditions, we can choose our attitude.
  • 3. Purpose is Powerful: Having a “why” can help us endure any “how.”

It’s a life-changing blend of psychology and philosophy that emphasizes hope and purpose in adversity.
