The Intelligent Investor by Benjamin Graham

The Intelligent Investor is a timeless guide to value investing. Benjamin Graham, the mentor to Warren Buffett, emphasizes the importance of discipline, patience, and a long-term mindset in building wealth through the stock market.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Margin of Safety: Invest only when there’s a significant difference between a stock’s value and its price.
  • 2. Mr. Market: Don't let the market’s irrational behavior influence your decisions.
  • 3. Investor vs. Speculator: Intelligent investors analyze businesses for long-term value, not quick profits.

This book is essential for anyone looking to understand investing fundamentals and build a strong financial foundation.
