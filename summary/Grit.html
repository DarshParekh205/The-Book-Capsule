Grit by Angela Duckworth

Grit dives into the science behind passion and perseverance. Angela Duckworth argues that talent alone isn’t enough—grit is what separates high achievers from the rest.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Passion + Perseverance: Long-term dedication beats raw talent.
  • 2. Growth Mindset: Believing that abilities can improve with effort.
  • 3. Purpose and Practice: Combining meaning with consistent practice is powerful.

The book encourages readers to embrace setbacks and keep pushing forward with consistent effort and passion.
