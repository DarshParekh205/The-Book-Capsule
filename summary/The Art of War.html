The Art of War by Sun Tzu

The Art of War is an ancient Chinese military treatise that applies strategic wisdom to warfare, leadership, and even modern life. Its lessons are used in business, politics, and self-discipline today.

Key Takeaways:

  • 1. Know Yourself and Your Enemy: Self-awareness and understanding your opponent is the key to victory.
  • 2. Strategy Over Strength: Smart planning wins over brute force.
  • 3. Adaptability: Success comes from adjusting quickly to change and seizing opportunities.
